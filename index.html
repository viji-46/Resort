frequently asked questions

how to cancel?

first check the cancellation policy to see if there are any fees or deadlines. Then, contact the hotel or the booking platform you used to make the reservation, either by phone, email, or through their website. Provide your reservation details and ask about refunds or cancellation fees. Finally, request a cancellation confirmation and monitor your bank account for any refunds.

is there any vacancy?

To check if there is a vacancy for a hotel room, you can either visit the hotel’s website, use booking platforms like Booking.com or Expedia, or call the hotel directly. These methods will show you available rooms based on your travel dates and location

what are payment methods?

credit and debit cards, online payment platforms like PayPal, and sometimes bank transfers.

how to claim coupons codes?

you can usually enter the code during the checkout process on the hotel's website or a booking platform. Look for a “promo code” or “discount code” field, where you can paste the code. If the code is valid, it will apply a discount to your total.

what are the age requirements?

Age requirements for hotel room bookings typically vary by hotel, but most hotels require guests to be at least 18 years old to make a reservation. Some hotels may have higher age requirements, especially for certain types of rooms or properties. If you're under 18, you may need to have an adult make the booking or check with the hotel directly for their specific policies.
